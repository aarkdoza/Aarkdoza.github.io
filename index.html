Supply Chain

In today's volatile global environment, Supply Chain & Procurement are no longer just operational functions — they are strategic drivers of business resilience and competitive advantage. Key trends shaping the future:
• Resilient Supply Networks — diversifying suppliers, reducing dependency risks
• Digital Transformation — AI, predictive analytics, blockchain for better decisions
• Sustainable Procurement — integrating ESG criteria into supplier selection
• Data-Driven Decision Making — real-time data to optimise costs and manage risks The future of Supply Chain is not just about moving goods — it's about creating intelligent, resilient, and sustainable ecosystems.

MiM Journey

The hardest lesson my MiM taught me wasn't finance or strategy — it was decision-making. When SKEMA asked us to choose our M1 S3 specialisation, I chose AI for Managers in Lille — stepping into a new city and a future-driven field. Then: Gap year or new campus? I chose Paris. M2: specialisation, exchange, double degree, CFA? That's when it clicked. The MiM isn't just teaching us business. It's training us to decide under pressure, with limited information, and full responsibility. To every MiM student feeling confused right now — that confusion isn't weakness. It's preparation. 💡
